Here's a timeline of Albert Einstein's life

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Württemberg, Germany
  • Einstein attended elementary school at the Luitpold Gymnasium in Munich.
  • He attended the Switzerland Institute of Technology.
  • 1902 - He started working as a clerk in a Swiss patent office.
  • 1903 - He got married to Mileva Maric.
  • 1905 - Einstein had four papers published in the Annalen der Physik, one of the best-known physics journals of the era. Two focused on the photoelectric effect and Brownian motion. The two others, which outlined E=MC2 and the special theory of relativity, were defining for Einstein’s career and the course of the study of physics.
  • 1915 - Einstein completed the general theory of relativity.
  • 1921 - Einstein won the Nobel Prize for Physics for his explanation of the photoelectric effect.
